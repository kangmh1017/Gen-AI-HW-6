Pitch-o-Matic 5000

Refine your startup pitch in minutes with an AI investor lens that scores investability and flags risks.

Overview

Founders often receive vague feedback from AI tools. Pitch-o-Matic 5000 solves this by evaluating startup ideas using structured investor criteria such as market size, competition, and scalability.

Users receive an investability score, risk analysis, and improvement suggestions aligned with real investor thinking.

The system includes input forms, backend AI evaluation, and a dashboard presenting structured feedback.
